Department Of Electronics Engineering

Electronics Engineering Department

Established: 1972

Programmes Offered: 3-Year Regular Diploma

Annual Intake: 60 Students

Vision

To be a model Institute for providing academic and skill excellence in Electronics Engineering.

Mission

  • Impart quality teaching-learning-experience with state of the art curriculum.
  • Enhance Training, Industry Institute Interaction and Entrepreneurship development.
  • Encourage students to improve their Creativity and Innovation to find solutions to common needs of society in a simpler way.
  • Foster sustained interaction with the alumni, industries, organizations and other stake holders to stay relevant in the globalized environment.

Laboratories

  • Electronics Circuits Lab
  • Digital Electronics Lab
  • Analogue Communication Lab
  • Industrial Electronics and PLC Lab
  • Microcontroller and Interfacing Lab
  • PCB and SPICE Lab
  • Software Lab
  • Computer Hardware Lab

Career Opportunities

Diploma holders in Electronics Engineering have opportunities in various sectors, including:

  • Electronics Manufacturing
  • Telecommunications
  • Automation and Control Systems
  • Embedded Systems
  • Consumer Electronics
  • Public Sector Undertakings
